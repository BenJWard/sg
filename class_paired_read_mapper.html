A mapper for linked reads from a PairedReadsDatastore.

Supports partial remapping of unmapped reads or of a selection list.

void PairedReadMapper::remove_obsolete_mappings ( )

Mapping of paired end read files.

Reads are mapped through a unique k-mer index in process_reads_from_file. R1 and R2 are processed independently. R1 gets odds ids, R2 gets the next even id, so [1,2] and [3,4] are pairs.
